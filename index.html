Welcome to Word Games

Explore various word games and tools.


Word Builder is a much more rudimentary version of the one available at aerolith.org.

The hooks games are to help extend words one letter at a time. The three letter words one is pretty robust, but those words with many hooks may look a little cramped.

The two letter word hooks game dynamically adjusts when there a word has a ridiculous number of hooks. The page may be a bit slow to load - but be patient. I tend to change the zoom on my screen to make it easier to use. Sometimes the longest words may be slightly cut off (especially if 9 letter word) - it's too hard for me to fix.
